# Balancing

## Progression

XP required is `floor(100 × level^1.55)`. The level cap is 100. Each level increases Strength, Agility, Stamina, Intelligence, and Sense by one and grants five unspent points.

## Derived values

- Strength: +0.15 attack damage per point above one
- Agility: up to +20% movement speed
- Stamina: +0.4 maximum health per point above one, capped at +80
- Intelligence: +8 maximum mana per point
- Black Heart: +1000 maximum mana and +20 shadow capacity

## Daily quest

10 hostile kills, 1,000 sprinted blocks, 30 push-ups, 30 sit-ups, and 30 squats. Reward: full recovery, 3 stat points, 250 gold, 500 XP, and one Blessed Random Box.
